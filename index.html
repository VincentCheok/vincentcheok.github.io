About

A project & operations leader who builds the systems brands run on.

Snapshot

Vincent is a Singapore-based project and operations management leader with 37+ years of experience spanning luxury retail merchandising, advertising production, regional workflow systems, and global project leadership. He has built and led teams across Asia Pacific for globally recognized names — Apple, DHL, Johnson & Johnson, Nike, Eastman Kodak, Tag Worldwide and more — with a consistent track record of turning slow, fragmented workflows into fast, accountable operations.

Since December 2022, he has been working on a freelance and fractional basis, providing ad-hoc project management and operations supervision for a range of companies — following senior chapters consulting for a food-tech experience center rollout, leading creative operations at Bybit/WeChain Fintech, building out a regional studio for Konica Minolta/Indicia Worldwide, an on-site secondment with Apple South East Asia, and operations leadership at Johnson & Johnson Singapore.
