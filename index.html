About

A brand operator who ships in every market, every language, every time zone.

Snapshot

Vincent is a Singapore-based marketing and brand operations leader with over two decades running regional campaigns for globally recognized names. He has built and led cross-market teams across Asia Pacific, grown accounts from a zero base to multi-million dollar businesses, and opened more than 20 retail stores across four countries. Equal parts strategist and operator, he specializes in translating brand equity into on-the-ground execution across cultures, time zones and marketplaces.

Most recently, as Regional Manager for Marketing Campaigns (APEM) at DHL, he managed a 12-market Email Contact Strategy program, DHL's Global Integrated Advertising Campaign, and the regional Welcome Pack toolkit refresh — coordinating directly with country teams and global creative and media agencies.
