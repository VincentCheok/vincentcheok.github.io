About

A project & operations leader who builds the systems brands run on.

Snapshot

Vincent is a Singapore-based project and operations management leader with 26+ years specializing in creative production services, studio operations, workflow systems implementation, and regional campaign delivery. He has built and led teams across Asia Pacific for globally recognized names — Apple, DHL, Johnson & Johnson, Nike, Eastman Kodak and Tag Worldwide — and has a track record of turning slow, fragmented workflows into fast, accountable operations.

Most recently, he has been consulting on the launch of new experience centers for a sustainable/alternative foods company and a food & beverage ingredients corporation, following senior project and studio leadership roles at Bybit/WeChain Fintech, Konica Minolta/Indicia Worldwide, Apple SEA and Johnson & Johnson Singapore.
